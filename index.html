A BBQ Journey Like No Other

Welcome to Thin Blue BBQ! A catering and award-winning competition team based in the heart of Michigan with a passion for service and BBQ. Our team is driven by a shared love for bold flavors and the art of smoking. We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients to craft our award-winning BBQ. As a competition team, we bring our best to every event. We blend tradition and innovation to deliver mouthwatering results. From slow-smoked brisket to perfectly seasoned ribs, every bite showcases our dedication to quality and flavor. Our expertise doesn’t stop at the competition table — we also offer catering services for events big and small. Whether it’s a family reunion, corporate gathering, or wedding, Thin Blue BBQ will bring the same championship-level BBQ to your special occasion. We’re proud to serve our community and share our passion for BBQ. Let Thin Blue BBQ make your next event unforgettable with savory smoky goodness and a side of warm hospitality and service.
